About Storybook

Discover the fascinating history of Port-Louis with our children’s picture and storybook. This book is perfect for children aged 8-12. The book will take you some centuries back to discover the capital during the French and British period. If you are curious to learn about Port-Louis in the past, this book is the perfect choice for you!
